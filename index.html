Treat yourselves to a moment of unique beauty and tranquility where the stunning desert landscape meets the Dead Sea’s turquoise hue.

Vert Dead Sea is our new elegant and indulgent vacation and spa site. Adjacent to the beach of Dead Sea , Vert Dead Sea is located at the lowest place on earth, defined as one of the wonders of the world. Vert Dead Sea is in complete harmony with nature. Indulge yourselves with a vacation in our newly designed suites and hotel rooms facing this rarest of nature’s scenic vistas.

Enjoy our pampering spa experience with more than 60 treatment options, an abundance of wellness activities with an emphasis on health, fitness and rejuvenation, our unique swimming pool containing Dead Sea water, the sulfur rich health pool, saunas, and Dead Sea mud treatments.

Is hiking your hobby? Vert Dead Sea is conveniently near the starting point of hiking trails that are among the most beautiful in the country and the world, especially Nahal Ein Bokek (Bokek Springs) and the Ein Gedi Reserve.

Vert Jerusalem is one of Jerusalem’s highest points on the city’s skyline, offering vistas of Jerusalem’s neighborhood’s and historic sites. Enjoy Vert Jerusalem’s beautifully designed suites and executive rooms, restaurants, the lobby’s international atmosphere, a stunning business lounge on the 21st floor, and a modern complex of events and conference halls.

Indulge yourselves, relax and de-stress at Vert Jerusalem’s spa center with our body balancing and wellness treatments.

Conveniently close to the International Convention Center, Vert Jerusalem is also an easy walk from the museum complex, Israel's Knesset (Parliament) and the Supreme Court. Also nearby are the charming alleys of the Nahlaot neighborhood and Mahane Yehuda, vibrant marketplace by day and pulsating hub of entertainment by night. You’ll enjoy our location at the city’s main entrance and an easy 5 minute walk from the fast train to Tel Aviv.
